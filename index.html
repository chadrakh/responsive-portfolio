    • About Me
    I'm a self-taught programmer, specifically working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React. I studied Graphic Design at Nottingham Trent University where I first took an interest in user interfacing during an app building project, which led to me diving into front end development and client-side programming. Front end development, for me, is a way of combining graphic design and code by allowing me to not only design interfaces but also build them up from scratch - the main reason I took a liking to it.
    As much as I love coding I also play basketball, design graphics (usually sports related) and binge a concerning amount of anime. From basketball I've also developed an athlete's mindset of always looking to work on my craft and accepting setbacks as a way to learn and grow from the experience.
